Our Story: The Delight of Puto

At *Puto Delights*, our mission is to share the authentic taste of the Philippines, starting with the beloved *Puto*. We honor the traditional process of perfectly steamed, fluffy rice cake while infusing modern, vibrant flavors like Ube and classic. Every bite is a commitment to quality, heritage, and the joy of sharing a Filipino classic.
